Kent Island only has three hotels; Holiday Inn Express, Hilton Garden Inn, and Best Western.  We have reserved blocks of 10 to 15 rooms in each of them and the rooms are first come, first served.  If you are looking for cheaper options, Annapolis is across the Chesepeake Bay Bridge and is roughly a 30-minute drive to the wedding venue.  Keep in mind that the shuttle service we are renting will not travel to any hotels other than the three on Kent Island.  There are also VRBO options on the island that are typically a little cheaper if you have enough people to share the cost with.
